Strategic Growth Recommendations
Based on 4-year Superstore data analysis. Prioritized by revenue impact and profit improvement potential.
✦ Opportunity
Technology is the profit engine. Despite being #1 in revenue at $836K, it delivers a 17.4% margin — nearly 7× higher than Furniture. Prioritize expanding the Technology catalog, especially Copiers (37.2% margin) and Accessories.
⚠ Problem
Tables sub-category is bleeding cash. $207K in revenue but –$17.7K in losses. The discount strategy applied to Tables (avg 45%) is destroying value. Immediate pricing review required.
→ Action
Central region is underperforming. With only 7.9% margin vs 14.9% in the West, Central leaves significant profit on the table. Review pricing, product mix, and discount practices in Central.
◎ Trend
Q4 seasonality is a major lever. November consistently drives 2–3× average monthly revenue. Build Q4 inventory strategy, pre-negotiate supplier terms, and launch loyalty campaigns by October.

Profit Improvement Actions

🪑
Reprice or discontinue Tables
Tables generated –$17.7K loss on $207K revenue. The 45% average discount is unsustainable. Either reprice above cost or remove low-margin SKUs.
